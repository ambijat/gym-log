Privacy Policy

DS5 Workout Log
This policy describes the data practices for the DS5 Workout Log web app and the Android Trusted Web Activity (TWA) distributed through Google Play.

Effective date: June 18, 2026

1. Overview

DS5 Workout Log is a workout tracking app that lets you log exercises, review history, export your entries, and import workout data files. The app is designed to work without requiring an account.

2. Data we collect

We do not intentionally collect personal data on our own servers.

  • Workout entries that you create inside the app.
  • Import files or shared text that you choose to open in the app.
  • Basic technical information handled by the hosting platform and standard browser/Android platform services.

3. How data is stored

Your workout log is stored locally in your browser/device storage (for example, localStorage) so that it can persist between sessions on the same device.

If you clear the app data, browser storage, or reinstall the app, your locally stored workout history may be deleted.

4. How data is used

  • To display your workout history and summary screens.
  • To export your data in CSV or JSON format when you choose to do so.
  • To import workout logs from files or shared text that you provide.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your workout log with third parties for advertising purposes. Data leaves your device only when you choose actions such as export, email, file import, or sharing through system features you initiate.

6. Third-party services

The app may rely on third-party services that are part of the web and Android ecosystem, including:

  • Google Play services and Google Play Console for distribution and app integrity.
  • GitHub Pages for website hosting.
  • Your browser and Android platform for storage, file handling, and sharing.

7. Permissions

The app does not request sensitive permissions for core workout logging. If you use optional Android features like notifications or file handling, those are limited to the functionality you choose to use.

8. Children

DS5 Workout Log is intended for general fitness use and is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, contact us so we can help you remove it where possible.

9. Data retention

Workout logs remain on your device until you delete them or clear app/browser data. We do not maintain a separate server-side copy of your workout history.

10. Security

We use standard platform protections and HTTPS hosting. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we do not intentionally keep a central copy of your workout log on our servers.

11. Your choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can delete your local app data from your browser or device.
  • You can export your data for your own records.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if the app changes. The latest version will always be published on this page.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app developer through the Google Play listing or the project owner channel you already use for this app.
